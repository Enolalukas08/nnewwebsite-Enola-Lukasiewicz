About Me

When I came to Paris, I wanted to discover a lot of things and to work in different fields of expertise. Indeed, during these last 3 years, my professional and personal experiences have given me the opportunity to develop my sense of rigor, pragmatism and teamwork, in addition to computer, design and management tools. Spontaneous, invested, and dynamic, I am ready to be part of your team.

A taste for challenge and fieldwork, interpersonal skills and service excellence are the main skills that have allowed me to take care of many clients while offering them in-depth and expert advice in brand storytelling, cosmetics and perfume. During this experience in retail, I was able to deepen my English and demonstrate a strong attention to detail and sensitivity to the world of fragrances.

Creative, persevering and autonomous, during my experience in Digital Marketing and Communication, I developed and executed the marketing plan for the launch of one of the company's board games. This mission increased the sales of the product by 50% on July 2021. As a creative force, I created augmented reality filters to promote a product during the 2021 Pink October campaign.

These skills, combined with self-control and initiative, will be a definite asset for your company in the management of a department in constant evolution.
